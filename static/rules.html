How payouts work

Everyone's money goes into one pool. When a competitor wins, the people who bet on them split the entire pool, divided by their effective bet.

effective bet = amount × multiplier your payout = (your effective bet on winner ÷ total effective bets on winner) × pool

Back the winner alone and you take everything. Several backers? You split it by effective bet.

The multiplier — why timing matters

Your bet is weighted by when you place it. Earlier bets earn a higher multiplier, because you're committing with less information. One of two formulas is chosen at setup:

Formula A — Linear Decay (gentle)

multiplier = 2 − (current_round − 1) / (total_rounds − 1)

Runs from 2.0× in round 1 down to exactly 1.0× in the final round, no matter how many players.

Formula B — Token (steep)

multiplier = competitors_remaining − 1

Starts at (players − 1)× in round 1 and falls to 1× at the final two. Rewards early commitment much harder.

A $20 bet in round 1 is worth $40 effective under Linear, or $140 under Token. A $20 bet in the final round is worth exactly $20 under either formula — no bonus once you have full information.

"Odds" on the tracker

The implied odds just reflect where the money currently sits — share = a competitor's effective bets ÷ all effective bets. It's the crowd's opinion, not a separate payout rule.
